Decision Points

For Decision Points our group needed to create an interactive based on our research topics choosen. Our topics were the Cuban Missile Crisis, Gangs, the Space Race, and 1870's Marketing.

For our interactive we created a card game that was unlike any other. It had similar aspects to popular card games today but added in the history elements so subtlety that you not only learned about the events but had fun while doing so.

The different decks for our card game were the Space Race, Cuban Missile Crisis, and Gangs while the marketing perspective overarched into all of the games. Each game had slightly different objectives or ways they were played but in the end they were all very similar.

Ontop of that we also had to make sure that people knew the rules so for each deck we included a different rules book. If you would like to see them you can access them below.
