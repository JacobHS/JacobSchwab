KaChing

For KaChing we had to create 3 products and then sell them as if we were a start up buisness. This was really hard to do and really pushed our skills to the test.

This was a really hard project and in the end it worked out but getting to there really pushed our skills and our trust in each other.

For our product we decided to create a coffee shop. The booth was very open and basic but the window display was quite advanced. I created a seesaw that moved autonomously and even used multiple strings of lights along with flood lights to light it up so people could see it at night.

Additionally the frame for the window display were made completely out of wood in a few days by me mainly with the help of my group members. They came out amazing and once the fabric was attached the whole booth came together as a whole.
